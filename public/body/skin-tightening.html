About Skin Tightening

Laser Skin Tightening uses leading laser technology to heat the skin surface to 41 degrees - 65 degrees underneath the skin in the epidermis/dermis layer to promote collagen production. The heat will then metabolise unhealthy fibres and cells causing stimulation to the fibroblast cells (these produce collagen and elastin fibres).

Skin tightening improves your skin’s elasticity and promotes the production of collagen. Your skin will feel firm and taut and lines will be reduced. A popular and effective treatment for stretched post-natal skin, aged skin tone and sagging facial tissue. Treatment areas include the face, tummy, breasts, chest, buttocks, thighs or neck.

The procedure is quick and comfortable, with minimal downtime.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question not listed below? Send us your Question to find more answers and info about our complete range of laser, skin, and cosmetic procedures.

  • How many skin tightening treatments are required for best results?

    For optimum results, we recommend a minimum of 4-6 treatments done at monthly intervals. Maintenance treatments are recommended to upkeep results. Our experienced team of Skin Therapists will tailor a treatment plan to deliver on your skin goals.

  • Is skin tightening treatment suitable for pregnant or breastfeeding women?

    We will require medical clearance for pregnant and breastfeeding individuals prior to proceeding with the treatment.

  • What areas of the body can be treated with Skin Tightening?

    This treatment can be done on most body areas. The most common areas include the face, neck, décolletage, and stomach.

  • How does Skin Tightening work?

    Laser Skin Tightening is designed to stimulate dermal collagen production and help with the regeneration of damaged skin cells. Skin tightening works on both Type 1 + Type 3 collagen which is a fibrous protein, found in the extra cellular matrix (Dermis). This treatment involves heat energy being delivered into the skin to penetrate deep down whilst protecting the upper visible layers and keeping them intact. It does this by deep thermal effect within the dermis causing collagen contracture and denaturation. This will then induce growth factors and stimulate new collagen synthesis which will then replace the old collagen with new fibres that are more compact and organized to give a less wrinkled appearance. Results are seen 3 months post treatments.
