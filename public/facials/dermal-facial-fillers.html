About Dermal Facial Fillers

Dermal fillers also known as injectable fillers, injectable cosmetic fillers and injectable facial fillers are soft tissue fillers which are injected into the skin to either fill in the lines or add volume to a shrunken area. Dermal fillers are most commonly used as a treatment for facial wrinkles, dark circle treatment (where dark circles have been caused by the hollow bone structure around the eye) and for getting the perfect pouts for the lips.

Dermal fillers are minimally invasive procedures performed by expert dermatologists to temporarily manage early skin aging issues to deliver a more youthful appearance. Fillers are composed of natural or synthetic substances in the gel form which when injected into the facial skin can diminish fine lines and restore fullness and volume to the face. The most widely used ingredient is hyaluronic acid that can instantly hydrate the skin and restore the skin to its earlier vibrancy by adding immediate skin volume.

Others are all collagen inducers like calcium hydroxyapatite, polyalkylimide, poly-L-lactic acid, and polymethyl-methacrylate microspheres (PMMA) are also popularly used fillers to reverse signs of aging.
