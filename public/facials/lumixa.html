About LUMIXA

Lumixa is the latest innovation in fluorescent light treatment, providing rejuvenating results through a process known as fluorejuvenation. Lumixa involves applying a gel with a specific energy profile to the skin, which is then activated with a blue LED light. When the gel is illuminated under the LED light, it converts the light into fluorescent light energy, which stimulates the skin’s natural healing mechanism. Lumixa is ideal for stimulating regeneration mechanisms in the skin, reinforcing natural defences, giving a natural glow, reducing redness, and stimulating production of collagen. There are two types of Lumixa treatment available:

Lumixa Skin Normaliser – This treatment gel has a specific energy profile that makes it ideal for targeting ageing skin, inflammation, dehydration and dullness. It can be used at any age as either a preventative or corrective treatment.

Lumixa Collagen Booster – This collagen boosting gel has a specific energy profile designed to target the signs and symptoms of ageing for those aged 35 and above. As well as increasing collagen production, this gel assists with reactivation of the skin matrix while also stimulating fibroblasts.

Preparation and aftercare

Prior to treatment:

Lumixa treatments are non-invasive and there is minimal preparation. You’ll wear goggles to block-out and protect your eyes from the LED light panels. During the treatment you will feel tingling and warmth as the gel works with the light – this is totally normal.

If one or more of the below apply to you, please let us know: Topical acne medications
Light sensitising medications
Pregnancy and breastfeeding

With no downtime, Lumixa allows you to go back to your daily life straight after the session.

What you’ll feel post-treatment:
Mild erythema
Temporary bronzing
Possible temporary darkening of freckles and sunspots

Immediate aftercare:
You’re good to commence the use of hyaluronic acid and all actives immediately after your treatment Minimize exercise, hot showers, pools spas and saunas for 24 hours post-treatment Use SPF at all times to protect your skin.

Lumixa treatment involves 5 treatments over 5 weeks. Results vary depending on the treatment protocol and skin condition. Some clients see an improvement in redness after just one single session. However, as Lumixa stimulates the skin’s biological processes, you’ll notice your best results after 12 weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question not listed below? Send us your Question to find more answers and info about our complete range of laser, skin, and cosmetic procedures.

  • IS LUMIXA TREATMENT RIGHT FOR ME?

    While Lumixa treatment is suitable for a range of skin types, it’s particularly beneficial for ageing skin. It can also target other skin concerns such as redness and inflammation. Ultimately, the right treatment for you will depend on your specific skin goals and concerns. When you arrange a consultation at our clinic, we will assess your skin and determine if Lumixa treatment is suitable for you.

  • What Is The Treatment Protocol?

    The standard protocol is 1 weekly session for 5 weeks. Lumixa treatments are non-invasive and suitable all year-round. Minimal to no downtime and make-up can be applied immediately after the session.
    A specific gel for each indication is applied.
    The gel remains on the skin surface and is not absorbed.
    The gel is activated with the use of an LED lamp between 10 to 20 minutes.
    The gel lights up and produces Fluorescent Light Energy which penetrates into the skin and stimulates cells.

  • How Long Until I See The Results?

    The treatment involves a course of 5 treatments weekly over 5 weeks, however, you’ll notice your best results after 12 weeks.

  • Do Lumixa Treatments Hurt?

    Lumixa treatments are non-invasive. During your treatment you may feel a tingling, warm sensation – it’s relaxing and enjoyable. Post-treatment, there is no downtime, you can even apply make-up and active skincare products straightaway!

  • What Is Fluorescent Light Energy (FLE)?

    FLE is a hyper-pulsed light, targeting multiple layers of the skin at once. FLE stimulates biological processes in the skin to activate the skin’s own regeneration methods. It’s amazing for controlling and minimising redness, producing collagen, and enhancing the skin’s glow, bringing dull skin back to life!

    Lumixa gel contains a patented fluorophore – a chemical compound excited by light. It’s effective at settling skin reactivity and stress, preventing and combating inflammation, and regenerating and revitalising the skin.
